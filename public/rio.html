Rio Carnival

The Greatest Show on Earth

Samba, Sun & Spectacle

Rio Carnival is the world's largest carnival festival, attracting millions to Brazil each year. Experience the electrifying energy of samba schools competing in the Sambadrome, vibrant street parties (blocos), and the infectious rhythm of Brazilian culture. This explosion of color, music, and dance transforms Rio de Janeiro into the planet's most exciting destination for five unforgettable days.

Why Experience Rio Carnival?

More than just a party, Rio Carnival is a cultural phenomenon showcasing Brazil's rich heritage through elaborate costumes, mesmerizing floats, and world-class samba performances. It's a celebration of life that will awaken all your senses and leave you with memories to last a lifetime.

Journey Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Rio

Settle into your accommodation, explore Copacabana Beach, and enjoy a welcome caipirinha as you prepare for the festivities.

Day 2: Sambadrome Spectacle

Witness the spectacular main parade with elaborate floats and thousands of performers competing for carnival glory.

Day 3: Street Party Experience

Join the legendary blocos (street parties), visit Christ the Redeemer, and experience Rio's nightlife.
