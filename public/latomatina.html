La Tomatina Festival

The World's Biggest Food Fight

Juicy Adventure in Spain

La Tomatina is an iconic festival held in the small town of Buñol, Spain, where thousands of participants gather to throw tomatoes in a friendly food fight. What began as a local event in 1945 has grown into an internationally renowned celebration of fun, food, and Spanish culture. Join the sea of red as the streets transform into rivers of tomato pulp, followed by music, dancing, and unforgettable memories.

Why Experience La Tomatina?

This unique festival offers more than just tomato throwing - it's a celebration of Spanish culture, camaraderie, and pure joy. The energy is electric as people from all over the world come together for this one-of-a-kind experience that you'll remember forever.

Journey Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Valencia

Explore the beautiful city of Valencia, visit the City of Arts and Sciences, and enjoy authentic paella by the beach.

Day 2: La Tomatina Festival

Early morning transfer to Buñol, participate in the epic tomato fight, then enjoy the after-party with music and celebrations.

Day 3: Relaxation Day

Visit Albufera Natural Park, take a boat ride, and unwind at Valencia's stunning beaches.
